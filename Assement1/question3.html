Tree planting holiday

Recently, due to the high rates of deforestation,and the incresaing rate of climate change,the government came up with a specific day where all its citizens can plant trees.

The main aim of this day is for all the citizens country wide to come together and plant trees in their specific areas of residence.
This thus increases the population oof trees countrywide and brngs about sense of patriotism among the citizens

the president himself acted as a role model to the entire nation by planting trees in different places in the capital city, in his home town and even in his own home.

The highest number of trees planted was recorded in schools and environmental supporting NGOs who combined planted ver 30,000 trees.
